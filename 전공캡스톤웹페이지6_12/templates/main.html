Cancer Metastasis Detector

Lymph nodes play a very fatal role in the metastasis of cancer, in the spread of cancer. Lymph node metastasis is the most common word
we hear when diagnosed with cancer at a hospital and what we should be interested in and treatment and prognosis depend on whether or not these lymph nodes metastasize.
Therefore, identifying the presence and stage of lymph node metastasis plays a very key role in treating and diagnosing cancer.

Prediction of lymphoid metastasis in breast cancer through breast cancer pathology slide images and clinical items
